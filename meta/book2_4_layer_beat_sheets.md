# Book 2 — 4-Layer Beat Sheets
## Target: 1,500–2,000 words per chapter

Each chapter must include **all four layers**.
If one is missing, the chapter will run short or feel mechanical.

---

## PROLOGUE — Audit Window Opened

### Physical Grounding
- Night camp outside the city
- Frost, silence, fire behaving incorrectly

### Interpersonal Friction
- Kellan pushes for strategy
- Lio anchors Rowan emotionally

### Internal Resistance
- Rowan’s fear of visibility
- Memory of Wildcard’s warning

### System Pressure
- Formal audit
- Deferred acknowledgment
- Countdown initiated
