## Step-by-Step: Publishing on Amazon KDP

### Phase 1: Pre-Publishing Setup

#### 1. Create/Verify KDP Account
1. Go to [kdp.amazon.com](https://kdp.amazon.com)
2. Sign in with your Amazon account (or create one)
3. Complete your **KDP Account Setup**:
   - **Author/Publisher Name**: Use your pen name (Jessica Braden)
   - **Primary Country/Region**: Where you'll receive payments
   - **Payment Method**: Bank account details for direct deposit
   - **Tax Information**: Complete W-9 (US) or equivalent for your country
   - **Phone Number**: Required for account verification

**Note**: Tax info can take 24-48 hours to process. Do this first!

#### 2. Prepare Your Files

**Manuscript File (.docx or .doc):**
- Use Word or similar
- Standard formatting (12pt font, 1.5 line spacing for readability)
- Chapter breaks with page breaks (Insert ‚Üí Page Break)
- Title page, copyright page, table of contents
- Remove headers/footers (KDP adds these automatically)
- Save as `.docx` (preferred) or `.doc`

**Cover File:**
- Format: JPEG or TIFF
- Dimensions: Minimum 1000 pixels on the longest side
- Recommended: 2560 x 1600 pixels (1.6:1 aspect ratio)
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- File size: Under 50 MB
- Color space: RGB

**IMPORTANT**: Your cover must include title, author name, and be appropriate for all ages.

#### 3. Write Your Book Description
- **Length**: 200-400 words ideal for LitRPG
- **Hook**: First sentence should grab attention
- **Formatting**: Use line breaks (Shift+Enter) for paragraphs
- **Include**: Genre tags, key themes, series info
- **Avoid**: ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation, misleading claims

**Example structure for "Fracture Protocol: Path of Unintended Consequence":**
```
[Hook paragraph - Rowan's situation and stakes]

[What makes it different - System as antagonist, SRE perspective]

[What readers will get - Found family, resistance, technical problem-solving]

[Series hook - Book 1 of Fracture Protocol series]
```

#### 4. Research Keywords
- **How many**: Up to 7 keywords/phrases
- **Strategy**: Mix broad and specific
- **For LitRPG**: Include genre + subgenre + themes
- **Examples for your book**:
  - LitRPG
  - Isekai
  - Female protagonist
  - System apocalypse
  - Dungeon crawler
  - Found family
  - Sci-fi fantasy

**Tip**: Use KDP's keyword research tool or browse Amazon bestsellers in your genre.

#### 5. Choose Categories
- **Primary Category**: Pick the most specific one (e.g., "Science Fiction & Fantasy > Fantasy > Contemporary")
- **Secondary Category**: Second best fit
- **Additional Categories**: Amazon may add more based on keywords and sales
- **For LitRPG**: Usually "Science Fiction & Fantasy > Fantasy" + "Science Fiction & Fantasy > Science Fiction"

**CRITICAL DECISION POINT**

**KDP Select Options**:
- ‚úÖ **Enroll in KDP Select** (recommended for your strategy)

  - Book available in Kindle Unlimited
  - Earn from page reads
  - Can use promotional tools (Free/Countdown deals)
  - Must be exclusive to Amazon (no other platforms)

  - Can sell on other platforms (Kobo, Apple, etc.)
  - No Kindle Unlimited access
  - No page read royalties

**For Your Strategy**: **Enroll in KDP Select** because:
- You want KU (Kindle Unlimited) access
- Rapid release series benefit from KU read-through
- LitRPG readers heavily use KU

## Pricing Strategy: Kindle vs Kindle Unlimited

### Understanding the Two Revenue Streams

#### Regular Kindle Sales (Non-KU readers)
- **How it works**: Reader buys your book for the set price
- **Your earnings**: 70% of sale price (minus delivery costs for books over 10MB)
- **Price range for 70%**: $2.99 - $9.99
- **Delivery costs**: ~$0.15 per MB for books over 10MB
- **Example**: Book priced at $4.99 = ~$3.49 per sale (70%)

#### Kindle Unlimited (KU subscribers)
- **How it works**: KU subscribers can read your book "for free" (they pay subscription)
- **Your earnings**: Paid per page read (Kindle Edition Normalized Page Count - KENPC)
- **Current rate**: ~$0.004-$0.005 per page (varies monthly based on KDP Select Global Fund)
- **Example**: 40,000 word book = ~160 pages KENPC = ~$0.64-$0.80 per full read

### Pricing Strategy for LitRPG Book 1

#### Recommended Price: **$4.99**

**Why $4.99?**
1. **70% royalty eligible**: Earns ~$3.49 per sale
2. **Market standard**: LitRPG Book 1s typically $3.99-$5.99
3. **Impulse buy range**: Low enough for discovery, high enough for quality signal
4. **Series positioning**: Signals this is a real book, not a quick cash grab

**Alternatives**:
- **$3.99**: More competitive, ~$2.79 per sale, good for aggressive marketing
- **$5.99**: Premium positioning, ~$4.19 per sale, if you have strong branding

**Avoid**:
- **$0.99**: Only 35% royalty (~$0.35 per sale), signals "low quality" to many readers
- **$2.99**: Minimum for 70% but can signal "cheap" to LitRPG market
- **$9.99+**: Too high for Book 1, 35% royalty, poor conversion

### KU vs Sales: What Matters More?

**For LitRPG Series (Your Strategy):**

**KU usually wins** because:
- LitRPG readers heavily subscribe to KU
- Series benefit from read-through (readers read Book 1-3+ in a row)
- Page reads can exceed sales revenue
- Better for rapid release (readers binge the series)

**Example Math** (simplified):
- **Sale at $4.99**: ~$3.49 one-time
- **KU full read**: ~$0.64-$0.80 one-time
- **BUT**: If 1 reader reads Books 1-3 via KU = $1.92-$2.40 total
- **AND**: KU readers are more likely to read multiple books in series
- **Result**: KU often generates more revenue for series authors

**Strategy**: 
- Price for sales ($4.99) to maximize per-sale revenue
- But optimize for KU read-through (series consistency, rapid release)

### Pricing for Future Books in Series

**Book 2-3**: **$4.99** (same as Book 1)
- Maintains consistency
- Signals equal value

**Book 4+**: Consider **$5.99**
- By this point, you have engaged readers
- Can command slightly higher price
- Still 70% royalty eligible

**Box Sets**: Consider later
- Books 1-3 bundle at $9.99 or $12.99
- Attracts new readers
- Additional revenue stream

## Key Terms to Know

**KDP Select**: Amazon's exclusivity program (90 days), includes KU access

**KENPC**: Kindle Edition Normalized Page Count - how Amazon calculates "pages" for KU payouts

**Royalty Rate**: 70% for $2.99-$9.99, 35% otherwise (for most territories)

**KU Read-Through**: When KU subscribers read multiple books in your series

**Rapid Release**: Publishing books in a series 4-8 weeks apart to maintain momentum

**Page Reads**: How KU authors get paid (per page, not per book)
