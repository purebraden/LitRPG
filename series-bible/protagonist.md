# Protagonist – Rowan Mercer

**Name:** Rowan Mercer  
**Age:** Early 30s  
**Background:** Software engineer with SRE / reliability overlap

Rowan is defined by her ability to keep systems running under stress—and her refusal to sacrifice people for metrics.

## Core Traits
- Observational, dry humor
- Pattern recognition
- Resistant to authority-by-design

## Flaws
- Burnout
- Over-responsibility
- Internalized guilt when systems fail

Rowan does not break systems by force—she exposes their failure modes.
