# Jordan Chen - Detailed Backstory and Arc

## Character Overview

**Name:** Jordan Chen  
**Age:** Mid-20s  
**Pronouns:** They/them  
**Background:** Junior software engineer, former colleague of Rowan  
**System Visibility:** Partial (can see some System interference)  
**Status:** Partially aligned but fighting it

## Pre-System Life

### Early Career

**Education:**
- Computer science degree
- Focus on systems engineering
- Good at optimization, efficiency
- Less experienced than Rowan

**First Job:**
- Joined Rowan's team as junior engineer
- Worked under Rowan's mentorship (informal)
- Learned systems thinking from Rowan
- Admired Rowan's ability to keep systems running

**Relationship with Rowan:**
- Respectful but not close
- Learned from Rowan's approach
- Saw Rowan's burnout (worried)
- Didn't know how to help

**Relationship with Maya:**
- Worked with Maya (senior engineer)
- Saw Maya's frustration
- Understood Maya's reasons for leaving
- Felt guilty (couldn't help)

### The Team's Decline

**After Maya Left:**
- Team became more stressed
- More incidents, more pressure
- Rowan became more burned out
- Jordan felt helpless
- Tried to help but couldn't

**The Breaking Point:**
- Another major incident
- Team blamed (unfairly)
- Management pressure increased
- Jordan couldn't handle it
- Decided to quit (like Maya)

**Quitting:**
- Gave notice (professional)
- Felt guilty (abandoning team)
- But also: couldn't continue
- Found job at smaller company
- Hoped for better work-life balance

### New Job

**Smaller Company:**
- Less pressure (initially)
- Better work-life balance
- But also: less resources
- More responsibility (smaller team)
- Still stressful (different kind)

**Server Room Incident:**
- Routine maintenance (server room)
- Something went wrong (similar to Rowan's fall)
- Reality fractured (different failure point)
- Fell through (like Rowan)
- Arrived in System world
