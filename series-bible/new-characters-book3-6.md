## Cohort Additions

### River
**First Appearance:** Book 3, Chapter 5  
**Archetype:** The Tempted  
**System Visibility:** Standard (cannot see System text)

**Key Traits:**
- Young person (early 20s)
- Struggling with resistance (exhausting)
- Tempted by safety (wants peace)
- Considers alignment (just wants relief)
- Joins cohort after choosing resistance

**Book 3 Arc:**
- Introduced as someone tempted by safety
- Goes to optimization clinic (aligns)
- Returns "improved" but remembers choosing
- Regrets alignment but can't fully undo it
- Partially recovers with Rowan's help
- Chooses resistance despite cost

**Character Arc:**
- Temptation → Alignment → Regret → Partial Recovery → Resistance

**Purpose:**
- Represents the temptation of safety
- Shows that alignment can feel like relief
- Demonstrates that recovery is possible but difficult
- Adds complexity to the safety vs freedom theme

**Critical Details:**
- Their alignment struggle shows the cost of safety
- Their partial recovery shows that healing is possible
- Their choice to resist shows that resistance is worth it

**Why This Works:**
