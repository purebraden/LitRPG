---

### ⚠️ Issue 2: Nova's Recovery Timeline

**Description:**
- Nova rescued in Book 4, Chapter 8
- Traumatized, recovers slowly
- **Book 4 refinement:** "Nova's trauma is a long tail (don't resolve it here)"
- **Question:** When does Nova "fully recover" (Book 6 says "fully recovered")?

**Current Status:**
- Book 4: Rescued, traumatized (trauma persists, long tail)
- Book 5: Recovering (mentioned)
- Book 6: "Fully recovered" (Chapter 18) - **CONTRADICTS Book 4 refinement**

**Recommendation:**
- **Fix:** Change Book 6, Chapter 18 from "Fully recovered" to "Recovered enough to function" or "Trauma has healed but scars remain"
- **Maintain consistency:** Nova's trauma should have lasting effects (like Rowan's empathy cost)
- **Better phrasing:** "Nova has recovered enough to function, but trauma scars remain" or "Nova's healing is ongoing, but she's functional now"

**Priority:** High (contradicts established refinement)

---

### ⚠️ Issue 3: System Message Frequency Post-Reformation

**Description:**
- Book 6 refinement: "System says less near the end"
- **Question:** How much less? Complete silence or just reduced?

**Current Status:**
- Book 6, Chapter 12: System says less (refinement applied)
- Book 6, Epilogue: System message appears (contradiction?)

**Recommendation:**
- **Fix:** Epilogue System message should be minimal or absent
- **Better:** Epilogue shows System silence (or very minimal/ambiguous message)
- **Alternative:** Epilogue message is ambiguous/unclear (feels like growth, not communication)
- **Best:** No System message in epilogue (silence feels like growth)

**Priority:** High (thematic consistency - contradicts refinement)
